Route 03 · Disclosure

Request the private channel before sending details.

Ordinary email is not a confidential report intake. Use the initial message to confirm the product, recipient, and a suitable private route. Technical evidence can follow after the channel is agreed.

Recipient
info@cyberkareem.com
Suggested subject
Responsible disclosure channel request
Next action
Initiate disclosure safely.

Do not include exploit code, credentials, personal data, or unpatched technical detail in the initial message.

Disclosure expectations

  • Confirm the affected product or project at a high level
  • Establish the authorization and recipient context
  • Request a private channel before sending technical evidence
  • Share only the minimum evidence needed to establish impact
  • Avoid real-user data, persistence, and service disruption
  • Coordinate publication timing with the responsible maintainer
