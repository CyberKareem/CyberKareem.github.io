Publishing standard · 02

Publication dates stay visible. Material technical corrections are dated.

  • 01

    Every article keeps its publication date and permanent CyberKareem URL.

  • 02

    Hack The Box walkthroughs publish only after a dated machine-retirement check.

  • 03

    Reusable real-world secrets are removed; authorized-lab demonstration credentials may remain when they are part of the methodology.

  • 04

    Material technical corrections receive dated notes. Titles, summaries, formatting, safety redactions, and section labels may be edited for clarity.
