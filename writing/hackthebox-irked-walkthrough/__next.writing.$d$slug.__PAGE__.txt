The subsequent escalation of privileges involves the utilization of Steganography techniques to advance to the “djmardov” user level. Lastly, root access is obtained by exploiting a custom SUID binary “viewuser”.</p><figure><img src="/writing/media/1bd9314d0965a768953f5ac3.webp" alt="HackTheBox “Irked” Walkthrough, figure 1" width="1200" height="909" loading="lazy" decoding="async" /></figure><p>Let’s get started!
