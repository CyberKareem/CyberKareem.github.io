One of these three networks is mandatory. Then you can choose between the remaining two networks.</p><p>In the reporting phase, I used the <a href="https://www.offensive-security.com/wifu/OSWP-Exam-Report.docx" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Offical Offensive Security OSWP Report</a>.</p><figure><img src="/writing/media/f2823bdc12fe72d28d717e06.webp" alt="Offensive Security Wireless Professional (OSWP) Certification Review, figure 5" width="1200" height="936" loading="lazy" decoding="async" /></figure><h2 id="conclusions">Conclusions</h2><p>I enjoyed the whole course and the exam experience, and I would totally say the course is worth it.</p><p>Regarding this exam and labs, you should rely on your own lab to prepare for wireless attacks.
