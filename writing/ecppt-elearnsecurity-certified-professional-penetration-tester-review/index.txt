I used a whiteboard for mind-mapping purposes throughout the exam.</p><p>During the external penetration test, you’ll encounter multiple vulnerabilities. In the end, I found three different ways to get a shell on the underlying system.</p><p>You’ll have to pivot to other domains and subnets and exploit the vulnerabilities present.</p><h2 id="reporting">Reporting</h2><p>My report was 81 pages long, with a lot of screenshots, executive summary, report structure, contents page, etc. eLearnSecurity is clear on the reporting requirements.
